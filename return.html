Return Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2025

Returns & Exchanges

At Home Decor & More, your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 14-day return window for eligible items.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of the purchase or delivery date.
  • Products must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Clearance or sale items marked as final sale
  • Opened bedding, linens, or hygiene-sensitive items

How to Return

You may return your item in-store or by mail. For mailed returns, please contact us at returns@homedecorandmore.com to initiate the return process and receive instructions.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or defective.
